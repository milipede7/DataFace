Your Facebook Face Recognition Data Face!










generate

Click generate and your face data will be interpreted

as an abstract portrait.

















How to get to your Facial Recognition data:

https://www.facebook.com/ > Settings > Your Facebook Information > Download Your Information

Select whatever you want from Date Range, Format and Media Quality

Although it will help this portrait if your facial recognition data points are from "all time"

Click Create File

Wait around for an hour or so, maybe listen to an ASMR explanation of the Linux Kernel

Once you have your data, find the about_you folder and open the face_recognition file

There will be four numbers in this file: threshold_green, threshold_yellow, threshold_red, and example_count

I don't know what the threshold numbers are but the example count is based on the number of photos you are tagged in, the stock that has trained the FB AI to learn your face

The raw_data should be a base64 encoding, we won't be using it for now

The number of circles in the portrait is based on your example_count and the randomized circle colors are based on a hard-coded value plus your color thresholds

The default portrait is based on my own personal data
